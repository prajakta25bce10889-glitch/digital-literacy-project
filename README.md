PROJECT OVERVIEW-
This project was a part of the Digital Literacy course to understand and learn about using digital tools safely and effectively. In order to complete this project, we had to work through five tasks, one for each module of this course.
The five tasks are-

TASK 1-
We had to create a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva.
The infographic includes topics such as what digital literacy means, safe internet practices, professional online presence and useful digital tools for students .
Designed to spread awareness among students in a simple and visual format.

TASK 2-
 We had to create professional profiles on various digital platforms.
 It is important for students to display their skills and be able to have a professional online presence.  

TASK 3-
 We had to explore coding and collaboration platforms. 
We also had to solve a beginner level preoblem.
Also had to create a quiz using Google Form. About Digital Literacy Awareness.
Google Form link-https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdZr9FIPQTZbHpNz5jOtziorACqo0khTCcA3htO2D9WMiJHcA/viewform?usp=header.

TASK 4-
The aim was on how to do professional digital communication.
We had to write to emails and make a checklist of do's and don'ts for social media.

TASK 5-
We had to study cybercrimes such as UPI fraud, identity theft, and cyberbullying for a case study .
The case study had to consist of: what the crime is, how it typically happens (step-by-step), who is usually targeted, and what the consequences are.
And also had to create a 'Stay Safe Online' checklist with at least 8 specific, actionable prevention tips relevant to college students in India.
