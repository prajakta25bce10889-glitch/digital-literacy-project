Stay Safe Online 


THE PREVENTION CHECKLIST-


* Do not share your PINs,OTPs or passwords with anyone
* Do not click on unknown links
* Always verify UPI payment request before accepting the request
* Use trusted and verified apps only
* Always remember- receiving money doesn't require approving a request
* Applying two-factor authentication to personal accounts
* Checking bank statements regularly
* Avoid doing transactions while on public wi-fi


REPORT CYBERCRIME-
*  National Cyber Crime Portal - cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline- 1930
